My Design Process

A structured approach to creating effective designs

1

Research & Discovery

Understanding project goals, target audience, and competition through market research and client interviews.

2

Concept & Strategy

Developing creative concepts, mood boards, and strategic direction for the visual identity.

3

Design & Creation

Executing designs using industry-standard tools, creating multiple iterations for client review.

4

Feedback & Revision

Presenting designs to clients, gathering feedback, and refining based on collaborative input.

5

Delivery & Implementation

Preparing final files, providing style guides, and ensuring proper implementation across all mediums.
